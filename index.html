AI Risk Repository

Field Explanations

AI Risk database: Contains the extracted information from papers.

Causal Taxonomy: Classifies risks based on their causal factors.

Domain Taxonomy: Classifies risks into specific AI risk domains and sub-domains.

Key Fields:

  • Category level: Paper, risk, subcategory of risk, or additional evidence
  • Risk category & subcategory: Extracted text for risk categories and subcategories
  • Description: Description of risk or risk category
  • Entity: Whether the risk is due to AI system, Human, or other
  • Intent: Whether the risk is due to expected or unexpected outcomes
  • Timing: Whether the risk occurs pre- or post-deployment
  • Domain: One of 7 AI risk domains
  • Sub-domain: One of 23 AI risk sub-domains
